Literature Survay


Autism is neurodevelopmental conditions in autism set of characterized by social interaction and communication difficulties.This mainly affected parts in the brain that controls communication and body movements. ASD children have less activity in brain areas that controls attention. An unusually narrow, no eye contact, no responsiveness and repetitive interests etc. Autism Spectrum Disorder(ASD) cannot be clearly defined, there are genetic problems, exposure to certain drugs and chemicals during the pregnancy and advance age of mother and father are some significant causes An autistic child may have main core symptom interactions are,
 Communication Difficulties
 Social Interaction Difficulties
 Repetitive Behaviors
Most of the autism related researchers have been done for clinical aspects.There are researches are ongoing to treatment level, lack of tools that has developed under diagnosis of autism and pre-aggression detection for non-technical environment. There are autism treatment applications in the android play store and the apple app store. But they haven‟t used any specific identical methodologies autism screening.

Research Problem


According to the physicians and the researches done, detecting autism in early ages is very important, as it is a must to give proper treatments and training for the autistic children to improve the mental health and the physical abilities. ASD can be detected in children from the age of 18 months, but currently in Sri Lankan most of the parents are unaware about this disease and the children who are suffering grow up not knowingly. This is a serious issue for the country and currently several authorities like health ministry and private communities arrange Autism aware campaigns like walks, and publish television advertisements to make the people aware about this disorder. The rural areas of the country, there are less number of consultant pediatricians who are specialized in children treatments. This factor also has a major impact on early detection of the ASD. As an undergraduates of Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology, if we can develop an alternative solution for this issue it will be a huge benefit for the future of our country and worldwide.

Research Gap


The main objective of developing this application is to prepare a Single Autism pre-detection application which the prediction of possibility of presence of autism can be done from four different areas. Eye gazing, Voice Recognition, Questionnaire using data mining and Behavioral analysis are the major areas that are focused in this application.
In the eye gazing there are lot of improvements in technology. Most of them are not used in screening methods for autism. We are trying to apply this eye gazing method through a puzzle game application. Autistic children are more attractive to the games which are colorful and meaningful to them.
Researches regarding head movement detection when the child is being called by the parent has been already carried out. What differs this methodology from the previous techniques which were used to detect movement as mentioned above is that our application will not only detect head movement, but also we will be tracking the eye of the child when he reacts to the parent/guardian. We will also be considering the time duration that the child takes to respond.
In the previous research done about “Using data mining and machine learning technologies” , though they have been able to predict the possibility of presence of ASD in children using a manual technology. But here we have automated that procaess.
Using Voice pattern analysis for identifyng autism is also added in this application.

Methodlogy

Eye gaze

In current methodlogies usued in implemented Autism screening tools, eye gazing is not considered as an identification factor. We developed a puzzle game to the community(children) and identify the focus of the eye gaze pointer to the image And the child is given 10 attempts images wich are familiar for Autistic and non autistic children. Finally, output will bring the percentage in probability of the autism level considering age limit comparison with the normal child.

Questionnaire for Autism detection

Questionnaire consists of 21 questions. The user has to reply for the questions with Yes/No responess. The responses are sent to the classification modal and it will evaluate the result and out put the prediction result. By using this result user can identify wether the child has a possibility of having ASD or not

Voice Pattern Analysis

We thought of letting the parent or the guardian interact with the child while obtaining voice clips from the child in the process. After obtaining them, the voice clips are analyzed to identify the number of meaningful words and the words that are repeatedly said by the child. Also, the voice recording will send to a classifier model in order to obtain a prediction on whether the child has possibility of having autism or not.

Responsivness

The main part of this function is to detect the head movements coordinates and facial landmarks. The application will identify the head movement coordinates and identify facial landmarks of the child who responds to his /her name which is called by the parent. Responding time will be calculated by the application. Responding is calculated by taking the difference between face landmarks detection time and the starting time of the application. .
